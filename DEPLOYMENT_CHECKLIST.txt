## Domain Pointing (if needed)

### Method 1: Update Nameservers (recommended)
1. Go to Namecheap DNS settings
2. Update to your hosting provider's nameservers:
   - Namecheap Hosting: ns1.namecheap.com, ns2.namecheap.com
   - Hostinger: Check your panel for their NS
   - Bluehost: Check your panel for their NS
3. Wait 24-48 hours for DNS propagation

### Method 2: A Records (faster)
1. Go to Namecheap DNS settings
2. Update A record to your hosting IP
3. Ask your host for the IP address
4. Wait 2-6 hours
