### STEP 4: Deploy to Netlify
1. Go to: https://netlify.com/signup
2. Click "Sign up with GitHub"
3. Click "Authorize netlify" (connects to your GitHub)
4. After login, click "Add new site" ‚Üí "Import an existing project"
5. Select your GitHub account
6. Find and select `portfolio` repository
7. Click "Deploy site"
8. **Wait 2-5 minutes** - your site will build and deploy automatically! üöÄ

### STEP 5: Connect Your Custom Domain
1. In Netlify dashboard, go to "Site settings"
2. Click "Domain management"
3. Click "Add custom domain"
4. Enter: `rijultandon.engineer`
5. Netlify shows you 4 nameservers (they look like: dns1.p01.nsone.net, etc.)
6. **Copy all 4 nameservers**

### STEP 6: Update Name.com DNS Settings
1. Go to: https://www.name.com
2. Log in with your Name.com credentials
3. Find `rijultandon.engineer` domain
4. Click on it
5. Go to "DNS" or "Nameservers" section
6. Replace the current nameservers with Netlify's 4 nameservers you copied
7. Save/Update
8. **Wait 24-48 hours** for DNS to propagate
